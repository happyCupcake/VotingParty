Topic 2: Environmental Policy

1. How concerned are you about the effects of climate change?
2. Do you support government regulations aimed at reducing carbon emissions from industries and vehicles?
3. Should the government incentivize and invest in renewable energy sources like solar and wind power?
4. How important is the preservation of natural habitats and biodiversity to you?
5. Should the government prioritize the development of nuclear energy as a low-carbon alternative?
6. How do you view policies related to conservation and public lands, such as national parks and wildlife refuges?
7. Should the government impose restrictions on activities like fracking and offshore drilling to protect the environment?
8. How important is it for the government to invest in research and development of green technologies?
9. Should the government encourage sustainable farming practices and reduce the use of pesticides and herbicides?
10. Do you support global efforts to address environmental issues and collaborate with other countries to combat climate change?
