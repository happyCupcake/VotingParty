Topic 1: Government's Role and Size

1. To what extent do you believe the government should be involved in regulating businesses and industries?
2. Do you support government-funded social programs like healthcare and education?
3. How do you feel about government regulations on individual liberties, such as personal firearm ownership?
4. Should the government provide financial assistance to individuals in need, even if it means higher taxes?
5. Do you think the government should be involved in setting minimum wage standards?
6. Should the government invest in infrastructure development and public transportation?
7. How much should the government be involved in regulating the internet and online content?
8. Should the government implement policies to address income inequality?
9. Do you believe in a balanced federal budget, even if it means reducing government spending on social programs?
10. How do you feel about the government's role in protecting the environment through regulations and incentives?
