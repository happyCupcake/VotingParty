Topic 2: Economic Policy

1. What is your preferred approach to taxation?
2. Should the government provide financial incentives to businesses to stimulate economic growth?
3. How do you view government involvement in wealth redistribution, such as social welfare programs?
4. Should the government regulate labor markets to protect worker rights and benefits?
5. Should the government provide subsidies to specific industries, such as agriculture or renewable energy?
6. How do you feel about government bailouts for struggling corporations during economic crises?
7. Should the government implement tariffs and trade restrictions to protect domestic industries?
8. How important is reducing the national debt to you, even if it means cutting government spending?
9. Should the government play a role in regulating cryptocurrency and digital financial transactions?
10. Do you believe in free-market capitalism, or do you support more government control and regulation of the economy?
