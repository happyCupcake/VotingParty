Topic 4: Foreign Policy

1. How do you feel about the use of military force in international conflicts?
2. Should the United States prioritize diplomacy and international cooperation over military action?
3. Should the United States maintain a significant military presence in foreign countries?
4. How important is it for the United States to be actively engaged in international organizations (e.g., United Nations, NATO)?
5. Should the United States provide foreign aid to other countries, even if it means less funding for domestic programs?
6. How do you view trade agreements and globalization?
7. Should the United States be more focused on protecting its own interests or promoting democracy and human rights abroad?
8. How should the United States approach its relationship with countries like Russia and China?
9. Do you believe in international efforts to combat climate change and global environmental issues?
10. Should the United States play a leading role in mediating conflicts and promoting peace in regions of the world with ongoing conflicts?
